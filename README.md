# Odoo ERP Implementation for PT GlowChain Logistic Indonesia

## Project Overview

This project focuses on the implementation of an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system using Odoo 17 for PT GlowChain Logistic Indonesia, a B2B cosmetic supply chain and distribution company.

The ERP system integrates multiple business functions to streamline operational processes, improve supply chain management, and enhance organizational efficiency through a centralized platform.

## Company Profile

- Company Name: PT GlowChain Logistic Indonesia
- Industry: Cosmetic Supply Chain & Distribution
- Business Model: B2B Cosmetic Distributor
- ERP Platform: Odoo 17

## Modules Implemented

- Human Resources & Fleet Management
- Purchase Management
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Sales Management
- Inventory Management
- Accounting Management

## Business Process Overview

The business process implemented in this project covers:

- Customer lead management
- Sales quotation and order processing
- Vendor procurement management
- Inventory monitoring and validation
- Logistics and fleet operations
- Financial transaction recording and payment registration
